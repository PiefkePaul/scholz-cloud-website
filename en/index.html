About Me

I am Paul Scholz, an IT systems administrator and infrastructure specialist from Germany. With years of experience in enterprise IT environments, including Hyper-V and SCVMM platforms with hundreds of hosts and thousands of virtual machines, PowerShell automation, and VDI management, I bring deep technical expertise to every project.

At the same time, I am passionate about SEO, AI-assisted automation, and digital visibility for small and medium-sized businesses. I also run an extensive homelab with Docker, self-hosting, and modern DevOps tooling. My goal is to make complex topics approachable, whether the challenge is server infrastructure, search engine optimization, or the rollout of AI tools.
